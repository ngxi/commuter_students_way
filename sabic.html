SABIC Station - Riyadh Metro

Welcome Sign Up Sign In SABIC Station

SABIC Station is the nearest metro station to Imam Mohammad Ibn Saud Islamic University. It is the most convenient option for students commuting via the Riyadh Metro.

How to Reach

  • Accessible via the Yellow Line (Line 4) and the Purple Line (Line 6)
  • Located on Uthman Bin Affan Road
  • Close to King Khalid International Airport and King Abdullah Financial District

Discounts and Tickets

  • Students receive a 50% discount with university ID
  • Ticket options:
    • 2-hour pass: SAR 4
    • 3-day pass: SAR 20
    • 7-day pass: SAR 40
    • 30-day pass: SAR 140

Metro Line Maps

  • Yellow Line: Connects King Khalid International Airport to King Abdullah Financial District
  • Purple Line: Runs from An Naseem to KAFD via Imam Mohammad Ibn Saud University
