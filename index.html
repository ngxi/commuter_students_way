Welcome to the student web portal designed to help you navigate the city and your university using the Riyadh Metro system.

The Riyadh Metro is a state-of-the-art transportation network that makes it easier, faster, and more sustainable for students to commute.

This platform allows you to sign up, check metro maps, get station information like the closest one to your university (SABIC Station), and benefit from student discounts.

Enjoy exploring the metro system and make your daily commute simpler and more efficient!
